Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

1. Data Collection

Discord Flight Tracker does not intentionally collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Discord Data

As a Discord bot, we may receive Discord user IDs, usernames, and message content as part of normal Discord bot operation. This data is processed transiently to respond to commands and is not stored persistently.

3. Flight Simulator Data

Flight position and status data is sourced from Microsoft Flight Simulator and is not linked to any personal information.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

5. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change, this page will be updated with a new effective date.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, open an issue on GitHub.
